# Bracers of Defense
*Wondrous item, major, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/img/bracers-of-defense.webp#right)


While wearing these bracers, you gain a +2 bonus to AC if you are wearing no armor and using no [shield](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/shield.md).

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 156. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
