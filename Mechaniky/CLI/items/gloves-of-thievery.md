# Gloves of Thievery
*Wondrous item, major, uncommon*  



These gloves are [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) while worn. While wearing them, you gain a +5 bonus to Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) checks and Dexterity checks made to pick locks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 172*
