# Assassin's Blood
*Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 150 gp

A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 6 (`1d12`) poison damage and is [poisoned](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for 24 hours. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and isn't [poisoned](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Poisoned).

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
