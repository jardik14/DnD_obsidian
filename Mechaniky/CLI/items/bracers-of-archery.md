# Bracers of Archery
*Wondrous item, major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/img/bracers-of-archery.webp#right)


While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the [longbow](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/longbow.md) and [shortbow](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/shortbow.md), and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls on ranged attacks made with such weapons.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 156. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
