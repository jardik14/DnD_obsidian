# Elixir of Health
*Potion, minor, rare*  



When you drink this potion, it cures any disease afflicting you, and it removes the [blinded](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [deafened](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [paralyzed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), and [poisoned](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions. The clear red liquid has tiny bubbles of light in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 168*
