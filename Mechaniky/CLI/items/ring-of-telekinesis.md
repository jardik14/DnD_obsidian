# Ring of Telekinesis
*Ring, major, very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/img/ring-of-telekinesis.webp#right)


While wearing this ring, you can cast the [telekinesis](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/telekinesis.md) spell at will, but you can target only objects that aren't being worn or carried.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 193. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
