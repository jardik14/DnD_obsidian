# Serpent Venom
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 200 gp

This poison must be harvested from a dead or [incapacitated](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) [giant poisonous snake](/Mechaniky/CLI/bestiary/beast/giant-poisonous-snake.md). A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (`3d6`) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
