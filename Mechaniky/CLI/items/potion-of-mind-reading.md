# Potion of Mind Reading
*Potion, minor, rare*  



When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [detect thoughts](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/detect-thoughts.md) spell (save DC 13). The potion's dense, purple liquid has an ovoid cloud of pink floating in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 188. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
