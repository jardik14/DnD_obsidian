# Divine Favor
*1st-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Your prayer empowers you with divine radiance. Until the spell ends, your weapon attacks deal an extra `1d4` radiant damage on a hit.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (War Domain)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-war-domain.md); [Paladin](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)
