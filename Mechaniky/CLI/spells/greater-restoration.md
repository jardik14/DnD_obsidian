# Greater Restoration
*5th-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target's [exhaustion](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level by one, or end one of the following effects on the target:

- One effect that [charmed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Charmed) or [petrified](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Petrified) the target  
- One curse, including the target's attunement to a cursed magic item  
- Any reduction to one of the target's ability scores  
- One effect reducing the target's hit point maximum  

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 246. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span> and the Basic Rules (2014)*
