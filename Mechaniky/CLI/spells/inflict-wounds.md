# Inflict Wounds
*1st-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes `3d10` necrotic damage.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by `1d10` for each slot level above 1st.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Paladin (Oathbreaker)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-oathbreaker-dmg.md "subclass=DMG")
