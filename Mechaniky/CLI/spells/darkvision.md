# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has [darkvision](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/senses.md#Darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.
