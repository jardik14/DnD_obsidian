# Revivify
*3rd-level, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamonds worth 300 gp, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature that has died within the last minute. That creature returns to life with 1 hit point. This spell can't return to life a creature that has died of old age, nor can it restore any missing body parts.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Life Domain)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-life-domain.md); [Cleric](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Paladin](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)
