# Invisibility
*2nd-level, Illusion*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an eyelash encased in gum arabic)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

A creature you touch becomes [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) as long as it is on the target's person. The spell ends for a target that attacks or casts a spell.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid (Circle of the Land, Grassland)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-circle-of-the-land.md); [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-eldritch-knight.md); [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-arcane-trickster.md); [Sorcerer](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-sorcerer.md); [Warlock](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md); [Wizard](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md)
