# Lesser Restoration
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be [blinded](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [deafened](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [paralyzed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [poisoned](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Poisoned).
