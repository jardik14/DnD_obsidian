# Animal Friendship
*1st-level, Enchantment*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

This spell lets you convince a beast that you mean it no harm. Choose a beast that you can see within range. It must see and hear you. If the beast's Intelligence is 4 or higher, the spell fails. Otherwise, the beast must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be [charmed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Charmed) by you for the spell's duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional beast for each slot level above 1st.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Nature Domain)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-nature-domain.md); [Druid](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Ranger](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md)
