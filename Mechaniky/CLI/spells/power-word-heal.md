# Power Word Heal
*9th-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes over the creature you touch. The target regains all its hit points. If the creature is [charmed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Charmed), [frightened](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Frightened), [paralyzed](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [stunned](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Stunned), the condition ends. If the creature is [prone](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Prone), it can use its reaction to stand up. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**Classes**: [Bard (Magical Secrets)](/Mechaniky/CLI/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 266*
