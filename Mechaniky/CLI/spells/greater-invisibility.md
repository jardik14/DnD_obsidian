# Greater Invisibility
*4th-level, Illusion*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You or a creature you touch becomes [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) as long as it is on the target's person.
