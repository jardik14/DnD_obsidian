# Repelling Blast
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*  

**Prerequisite**: [eldritch blast](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/eldritch-blast.md) cantrip

When you hit a creature with [eldritch blast](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/eldritch-blast.md), you can push the creature up to 10 feet away from you in a straight line.
