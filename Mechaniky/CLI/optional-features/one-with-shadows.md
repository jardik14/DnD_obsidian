# One with Shadows
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*  

**Prerequisite**: Level 5

When you are in an area of dim light or darkness, you can use your action to become [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) until you move or take an action or a reaction.
