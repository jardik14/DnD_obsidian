# Charm of Feather Falling

This charm grants you the benefits of a [ring of feather falling](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/ring-of-feather-falling.md). These benefits last for 10 days, after which the charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228*
