# Boon of the Night Spirit

While completely in an area of dim light or darkness, you can become [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) as an action. You remain [invisible](/Mechaniky/CLI/Rules/conditions.md#Invisible) until you take an action or a reaction. 

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
