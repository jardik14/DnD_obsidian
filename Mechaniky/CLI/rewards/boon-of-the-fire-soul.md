# Boon of the Fire Soul

You have immunity to fire damage. You can also cast [burning hands](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/burning-hands.md) (save DC 15) at will, without using a spell slot or any components. 

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
