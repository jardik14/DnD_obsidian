# Boon of the Stormborn

You have immunity to lightning and thunder damage. You can also cast [thunderwave](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/thunderwave.md) (save DC 15) at will, without using a spell slot or any components.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
