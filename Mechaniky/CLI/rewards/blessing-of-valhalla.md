# Blessing of Valhalla

This blessing grants you the power to summon spirit warriors, as if you had blown a [silver horn of Valhalla](/Mechaniky/CLI/items/horn-of-valhalla-silver.md). Once you use this blessing, you can't use it again until 7 days have passed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228*
