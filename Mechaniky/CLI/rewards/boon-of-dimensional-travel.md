# Boon of Dimensional Travel

As an action, you can cast the [misty step](/Mechaniky/CLI/spells/misty-step.md) spell, without using a spell slot or any components. Once you do so, you can't use this boon again until you finish a short rest.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
